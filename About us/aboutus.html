About Us

Who we are? & Why we are?



Founded in the bustling streets of New York City in 1985, SPIN was the brainchild of visionary designer Miranda St. Clair. Known for her eclectic style and daring combinations, Miranda set out to revolutionize the fashion industry by merging avant-garde aesthetics with everyday wearability. SPIN's early years were marked by bold experimentation and rapid growth. Miranda's knack for predicting trends before they hit the mainstream earned the brand a cult following among fashion-forward urbanites and celebrities alike. By the early 1990s, SPIN had expanded internationally, opening flagship stores in Paris, London, and Tokyo, solidifying its status as a global fashion powerhouse. Throughout the decades, SPIN continued to push boundaries, pioneering sustainable practices long before they became industry norms. Miranda St. Clair's commitment to ethical fashion and her advocacy for fair labor practices set SPIN apart as a leader in corporate responsibility. In the early 2000s, SPIN launched its iconic denim line, which quickly became a staple in every fashion-conscious wardrobe. The brand's ability to blend classic styles with cutting-edge trends ensured its relevance in an ever-changing market. Today, SPIN remains at the forefront of the fashion industry, with Miranda St. Clair still serving as its creative director. Under her leadership, SPIN continues to innovate, embracing new technologies and materials to create collections that are as sustainable as they are stylish. With a loyal customer base spanning generations, SPIN looks toward the future with optimism, committed to shaping the next chapter of fashion history.
